ABOUT US

The Disciples is a next generation Non-Profit Organisations dedicated to forging a more resilient and dignified future for marginalized communities in South Africa. Born from the urgent need to address the complex web of poverty and social inequality, we employ a holistic strategy that moves beyond immediate aid. We focus on providing essential humanitarian support, like food and clothing, while concurrently implementing future-focused skills training programs. Our vision is a society where every individual, regardless of their background, possesses the self-reliance, skills, and dignity needed to lead a fulfilling life, thereby reducing crime and fostering long-term social and economic upliftment.
